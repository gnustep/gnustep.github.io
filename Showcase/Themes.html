Themes#

Based on other widget toolkits#

  • plugins-themes-Gtk – Uses your Gtk2 theme

  • plugins-themes-WinUXTheme – Uses Win32 (Windows only)

Normal themes#

NeXT style#

  • GNUstep – the default theme, somewhat similar to NeXT

  • Heritage (description) – very similar to NeXT

  • Neos (description) – a more subtle NeXT-style theme

  • Tango (description) – NeXT-style theme recolored to work well with Tango icons

Mac style#

  • NesedahRik

  • NarcissusRik

  • gershwin-rik-theme

Flat themes#

  • Sleek (description) – grey, completely flat theme

  • Hyconish

Dark themes#

  • Sombre – this is the most recent dark theme

  • ThinkDark

  • ThinkDark2

  • Darkness

Other#

  • WinClassic (description) – looks like Windows 2000
