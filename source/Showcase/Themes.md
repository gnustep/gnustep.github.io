## Based on other widget toolkits

* [plugins-themes-Gtk](https://github.com/gnustep/plugins-themes-Gtk) -- Uses your Gtk2 theme
* [plugins-themes-WinUXTheme](https://github.com/gnustep/plugins-themes-WinUXTheme) -- Uses Win32 (Windows only)

## Normal themes

### NeXT style

* GNUstep -- the default theme, somewhat similar to NeXT
* [Heritage](https://github.com/gnustep/themes/tree/master/Heritage.theme/) ([description](https://gap.nongnu.org/themes/index.html)) -- very similar to NeXT
* [Neos](https://github.com/gnustep/themes/tree/master/Neos.theme) ([description](https://gap.nongnu.org/themes/index.html)) -- a more subtle NeXT-style theme
* [Tango](https://github.com/gnustep/themes/tree/master/Tango.theme) ([description](https://lists.nongnu.org/archive/html/discuss-gnustep/2015-11/msg00051.html)) -- NeXT-style theme recolored to work well with Tango icons

### Mac style

* [NesedahRik](https://github.com/gnustep/plugins-themes-nesedahrik)
* [NarcissusRik](https://github.com/gnustep/plugins-themes-narcissusrik)
* [gershwin-rik-theme](https://github.com/gershwin-desktop/gershwin-rik-theme)

### Flat themes

* [Sleek](https://github.com/gnustep/themes/tree/master/Sleek.theme) ([description](https://gap.nongnu.org/themes/index.html)) -- grey, completely flat theme
* [Hyconish](https://github.com/prateek-khandelwal/hyconish.theme)

### Dark themes

* [Sombre](https://github.com/gnustep/plugins-themes-sombre) -- this is the most recent dark theme
* [ThinkDark](https://github.com/gnustep/themes/tree/master/ThinkDark.theme)
* [ThinkDark2](https://github.com/gnustep/themes/tree/master/ThinkDark2.theme)
* [Darkness](https://github.com/gnustep/plugins-themes)

### Other
